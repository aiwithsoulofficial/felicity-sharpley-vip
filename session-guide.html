The Training

Catching the first whisper

Two practices, both tiny, both daily. As a 1/3 you build trust through your own evidence - so treat these as experiments and keep the results in your journal. That data set is how your mind finally lets your body lead.

Practice one: the sway test (your body as pendulum)

  1. Stand with feet hip-width apart, knees soft, eyes closed. Drop your attention into the belly.
  2. Calibrate first. Say out loud: "My name is Felicity." Feel the body tip gently forward - that is your yes. Now say a false name. Feel the body tip back - that is your no.
  3. Once you can feel the difference, ask your real question. Clear yes/no questions only, stay neutral, and the FIRST movement is the answer. The moment the mind starts debating, you have left the tool.

Test it on small things daily - a food, a supplement, a choice. Small experiments, honest notes. Your own evidence, Investigator.

Practice two: the daily first-whisper catch

Once a day, catch a splenic hit BEFORE the mind speaks - which route to drive, which queue to join, which food to pick, whether to answer the phone. Act on it. Then journal what happened.

Your spleen has been talking your whole life. This practice is just turning your head toward it.
